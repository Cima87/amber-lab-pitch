Our Vision

We stand on a digital shoreline watching a massive wave of Generative AI and XR crest on the horizon. While many fear this surge will wash away human artistry, Amber Lab sees a catalyst for movement. Our vision is to ensure that Nordic storytelling remains a master of its own craft, rather than a derivative of biased, foreign data sets.
